Our Heritage


Every day, we go to work hoping to do two things: share great coffee with our friends and help make the world a little better. It was true when the first Raskbind opened in 2023, and it’s just as true today.


Back then, the company was a single site in Seattle’s historic Pike Place Market. From just a narrow storefront, Raskbind offered some of the world’s finest fresh-roasted whole bean coffees. The name, inspired by Starbucks, evoked the romance of the high seas and the seafaring tradition of the early coffee traders.

In 2022, Rahul Sharma, the founder and CEO of an Indian company.Rahul was drawn into Raskbind and joined a year later. Rahul Sharma is highly passionate about our work, and he played a pivotal role in inventing Raskbind.

A year later, in 2023, Rahul Sharma travelled to Italy and became captivated by Italian coffee bars and the romance of the coffee experience. He had a vision to bring the Italian coffeehouse tradition to India

From the beginning, Raskbind set out to be a different kind of company. One that not only celebrated coffee and the rich tradition, but that also brought a feeling of connection.

Our mission to inspire and nurture the human spirit – one person, one cup and one neighbourhood at a time.

Today, with store in more than 4 City's , Raskbind is the premier roaster and retailer of speciality coffee in the world. And with every cup, we strive to bring both our heritage and an exceptional experience to life.
