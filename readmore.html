Meet Altamash Mahmood

I'm Altamash Mahmood, the founder and lead video editor at AM Frame Works. My journey into the world of video editing began with a simple curiosity about storytelling through visuals. This curiosity quickly turned into a passion, and for the past five years, I've dedicated myself to mastering the art and craft of video editing.

My Journey

After completing my 12th grade, I dove headfirst into the dynamic field of video production. From early freelance projects to working with some of the most influential content creators, my career has been a blend of continuous learning and creative exploration. One of my most notable collaborations is with "Dear Sir," a YouTube channel that boasts over 18 million subscribers. This experience not only honed my technical skills but also taught me the importance of engaging storytelling.

Skills and Expertise

At AM Frame Works, we handle all types of video projects. My expertise spans across:

• Colour Grading: Enhancing visuals to create mood and atmosphere.

• Motion Graphics: Adding dynamic elements to keep viewers engaged. Sound Editing: Ensuring audio quality matches the visual excellence.

• Cutting and Splicing: Seamlessly weaving together footage to tell a compelling story.

I am proficient in industry-leading software such as Adobe Premiere Pro, Final Cut Pro, and DaVinci Resolve, ensuring that each project meets the highest standards of quality.
